About Doreli Board




Doreli Board is an online notice board service set up by 5 Ashesi Students.
This application acts just like the normal notice boards we see in our schools and institutions.
Organisations and institutions register and then put up their announcements on the page.
They are able to determine when an announcement should be taken off the board
and who can make changes to the announcements.
